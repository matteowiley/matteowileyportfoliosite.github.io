International Studies Abroad's mission is to provide high quality education abroad opportunities to college students from around the world. The Internship and Service-Learning project was the result of a merger between two companies and two separate departments. The challenges faced where keeping the brands equally balanced aesthetically and dynamically displaying the content in the most strategic way possible. As one of two Front-end Developers there were many accomplishments made such as mobile-first design, best practices and to the production of clean dry code.
